alt-tab	                switch between windows. Tap to go to the last window you used. Tap more than once to cycle through all windows
ctrl-s	                save
ctrl-c	                copy
ctrl-v	                paste
ctrl-x	                cut (like copy, but also deletes what you've selected
ctrl-z	                undo
ctrl-a	                select everything!
shift + left arrow  	selects the character to your left. Keep tapping to select more characters
F5	                    refreshes your browser page
